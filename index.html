ABOUT

Luis Cerdas Jaubert is a Costa Rican artist who was born in 1982 in San Jose. He completed a Bachelor's degree in Illustration and Printmaking at the University of Costa Rica in 2006 and a Master's degree in Animation and T.V.Graphics at the Academy of Art, Architecture, and Design in Prague(VSUP) in 2011.

Since graduating school, Jaubert has continued to create at a feverish pace, working in painting, printmaking, music, and exhibiting and performing across Europe. His work combines simple images strongly attached to his feelings with his own memories from the past, and weaves together expressive and conceptual elements.

His work removes parts of differing images from their original context and combines them with other ones to form new objects, a process that provokes emotional reactions in the artist, thereby creating a visual dialogue between new and old, artist and artwork. Most dissections involve the careful isolation and removal of individual cases, accessing the area in which the parts in question are situated and systematically removing them from their connections to their surroundings. However, the visual communication between the disparate elements in his work aims to use memories attached to a different images to reveal what "used to be" in the mind of the artist. This process creates new situations based on reminiscences inspired by the original images to produce a new feeling within a new image.
